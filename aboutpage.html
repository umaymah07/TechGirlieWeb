OUR STORY

TechGirlie began in 2007, born from living rooms where a few determined women defied norms in a world where we weren’t allowed to work in tech. With no offices or funding, we taught ourselves, supported one another, and built a sisterhood of skill and strength.

Now in 2025, we’ve grown into a global movement—empowering women in STEM, running successful businesses, and proving that no door is closed when women build their own.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to foster an inclusive, equitable environment where women can thrive in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

Through education, mentorship, and innovation, we aim to lead systemic change and global impact.

OUR VALUES

💧 Hydrate – We believe in balance—rest, recharge, and take care of yourself. Wellness fuels greatness.

⚡ Hustle – We show up. We level up. We build boldly and lead fiercely in every space we enter.

👜 Hire – We don’t just open doors—we hold them open. We hire, mentor, and champion women in STEM.
